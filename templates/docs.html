About LIP READER

LIP READER is a cutting-edge application designed to assist individuals with hearing impairments by converting lip movements into text using advanced artificial intelligence. This tool provides reliable support for communication needs.

How to Use

Using LIP READER is simple and straightforward:

  1. Upload a Video: Drag and drop an MPG or MP4 video file into the designated area.
  2. Automatic Processing: The AI will process the video and convert lip movements to text.
  3. Read the Text: The generated text will be displayed next to the video.
